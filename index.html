Introduction

"Sentiment Sorcerer: The EmoMagic Trial" is a 10-minute interactive virtual reality experience that puts players in the role of a candidate undergoing a trial. Throughout the experience, participants will learn about the power of emotions and words through examiner-led spell instruction, immersive storytelling, and practical spellcasting. The words you speak will serve as incantations to wield magic, making the experience emotionally charged and engaging.

This piece is based on our observations of human interactions in daily life. Through conversations with friends, family, spiritual talks, and counseling, we discovered the power of words to influence others' emotions, much like magic. Our goal is to bring emotions to life through words, allowing the audience to see the power within them.

Features

Three key features in the EmoMagic Trial.

Storytelling

Watch situational stories and give the storytellers the emotional words they need!

Sentiment Analysis

Using Speech-to-Text and Natural Language AI technology, positive and negative mood words will lead to different results.

Spellcasting Effect

Depending on the result of the spell, there will be corresponding auditory and visual experiences. All audio is self-produced.
