Introduction

"Sentiment Sorcerer: The EmoMagic Trial" is a 10-minute VR interactive story where participants assume the role of a candidate undergoing a magic trial. You'll learn about the power embedded in emotions and language through examiner-led spell instruction, immersive storytelling, and practical spellcasting.
Last but not least, the emotionally charged words you speak will serve as incantations to wield magic.

Inspired by observations of human interactions in daily life, the project recognizes the influential and contagious power of language, much like magic. Leveraging emotional recognition technology in language models, the goal is to visualize emotions, empowering everyone to become a sorcerer and understand the potency within emotional expressions.

Features

Three key features in the EmoMagic Trial.

Storytelling

Watch situational stories and give the storytellers the emotional words they need!

Sentiment Analysis

Using Speech-to-Text and Natural Language AI technology, positive and negative mood words will lead to different results.

Spellcasting Effect

Depending on the result of the spell, there will be corresponding auditory and visual experiences.
